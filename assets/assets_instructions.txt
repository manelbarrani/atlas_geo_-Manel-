Place your images into this folder with the following names:

- globe.png  -> use your file: C:\Users\DELL\Desktop\globe.png
- tunisia_big.png  -> use your file: C:\Users\DELL\Desktop\Tunisia_Big.png

Example (PowerShell):

Copy-Item "C:\Users\DELL\Desktop\globe.png" -Destination "$(Resolve-Path .)\\assets\\globe.png"
Copy-Item "C:\Users\DELL\Desktop\Tunisia_Big.png" -Destination "$(Resolve-Path .)\\assets\\tunisia_big.png"

After copying, restart Metro (`npm start`).
